# Summary

**$40-70B MENA market** with massive renewable energy transition. Saudi Vision 2030 and regional sustainability targets driving unprecedented investment. Solar installation and energy services are prime SME opportunities.

**Success Factors:** Technical certifications, safety record, renewable capability, project management
**Red Flags:** Poor safety, no renewable capability, single project dependency, old equipment
**Upside:** Solar expertise, long-term O&M contracts, energy efficiency capability
