# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**Market Characteristics:**
- $15-25B total MENA market
- Highly fragmented (many small workshops)
- Strong import competition (China, Turkey)
- Real estate/construction cycle dependent
- Vision 2030 driving hospitality demand

**SME Opportunities:**
- Kitchen/cabinetry (growing, less import competition)
- Hospitality/contract (Vision 2030 mega-projects)
- Custom/bespoke (relationship-based)
- E-commerce (digital disruption)

**Success Factors:**
- Quality consistency
- Design capability
- Delivery reliability
- Digital adoption (CNC, CAD)
- Project management for contracts

**Red Flags:**
- High material waste (>15%)
- Long lead times (>8 weeks)
- Low equipment utilization
- No CNC/modern equipment
- Poor quality control

**Upside Indicators:**
- CNC automation
- Design capability
- Contract/project relationships
- E-commerce presence
- Export capability
