# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**Market Characteristics:**
- $12-20B total MENA market
- Raw material dependent (resin prices)
- GCC has petrochemical cost advantage
- Growing sustainability pressure
- Import competition (China)

**SME Opportunities:**
- Food packaging (growing demand)
- Construction products (real estate boom)
- Technical/specialty parts (less competition)
- Recycling (policy-driven)

**Success Factors:**
- Raw material sourcing/relationships
- Machine efficiency (OEE)
- Quality consistency
- Customer relationships
- Cost control

**Red Flags:**
- High scrap rates (>8%)
- Low OEE (<55%)
- Single customer dependency
- Old equipment
- No food contact certification

**Upside Indicators:**
- Modern machinery
- Food/pharma certifications
- Long-term contracts
- Diversified customer base
- Sustainability capabilities
