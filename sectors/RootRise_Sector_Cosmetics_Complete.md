# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**$15-25B MENA market** with strong growth in halal, natural, and men's grooming segments. High multinational competition but opportunities in local relevance, halal certification, and contract manufacturing.

**SME Opportunities:**
- Halal-certified cosmetics
- Natural/organic products
- Contract manufacturing (Egypt)
- Local hero ingredients (argan, Dead Sea)
- Men's grooming
- Private label for hospitality

**Success Factors:**
- GMP compliance (ISO 22716)
- Formulation capability
- Halal certification
- Speed to market
- Brand building

**Red Flags:**
- No GMP certification
- Quality issues/recalls
- Registration gaps
- High SKU count without volume
- Dependency on single customer

**Upside Indicators:**
- ISO 22716 certified
- Own successful brands
- Halal certification
- Export capability
- R&D/formulation strength
