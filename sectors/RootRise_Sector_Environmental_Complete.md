# Summary

**$15-25B MENA market** with accelerating growth driven by sustainability regulations and circular economy initiatives. Saudi Vision 2030 targets 94% waste diversion. UAE targeting zero waste to landfill.

**Success Factors:** Environmental permits, operational efficiency, safety, sustainability certifications
**Red Flags:** Compliance issues, no permits, poor safety, environmental incidents
**Upside:** Recycling capability, industrial waste expertise, consulting credentials
