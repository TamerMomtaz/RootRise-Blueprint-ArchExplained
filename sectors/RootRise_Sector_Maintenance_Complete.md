# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**$20-35B MENA market** driven by real estate growth, industrial expansion, and Vision 2030 mega-projects. Shift from reactive to contract-based maintenance creating recurring revenue opportunities.

**SME Opportunities:**
- Building maintenance contracts
- Industrial equipment O&M
- HVAC services
- Specialized systems (elevator, fire)
- Predictive maintenance services
- FM subcontracting

**Success Factors:**
- High first-time fix rate
- Response time performance
- Technical capabilities
- Contract relationships
- Safety record

**Red Flags:**
- Low SLA compliance (<90%)
- High callback rate (>15%)
- No recurring revenue
- Poor safety record
- Unlicensed operations

**Upside Indicators:**
- High recurring revenue (>60%)
- OEM authorizations
- ISO certifications
- Strong contract retention
- Specialized capabilities
