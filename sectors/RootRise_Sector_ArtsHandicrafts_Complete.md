# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**$5-10B MENA crafts market** with strong heritage traditions and growing export potential through e-commerce. Tourism drives domestic sales, while digital channels enable global reach.

**SME Opportunities:**
- E-commerce export (Etsy, own site)
- Tourism product development
- Contemporary design adaptation
- Interior design/hospitality supply
- Heritage brand building
- Cooperative organization

**Success Factors:**
- Authenticity and quality
- Digital presence
- Story/brand development
- Export capability
- Design innovation
- Artisan skill preservation

**Red Flags:**
- Tourism-only dependency
- No digital presence
- Quality inconsistency
- No succession planning
- Low margins, commodity competition

**Upside Indicators:**
- Strong export revenue
- E-commerce capability
- Recognized artisans
- Design differentiation
- Sustainable practices
