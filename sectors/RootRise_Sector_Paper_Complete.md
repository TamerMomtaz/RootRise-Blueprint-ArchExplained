# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**Market Characteristics:**
- $10-15B total MENA market
- Commercial print declining
- Packaging print growing
- Digital disruption ongoing
- Overcapacity in commercial

**SME Opportunities:**
- Packaging printing (e-commerce growth)
- Digital/short-run (personalization)
- Labels (product proliferation)
- Web-to-print services

**Success Factors:**
- Equipment utilization
- Speed/turnaround
- Quality consistency
- Customer relationships
- Digital capability

**Red Flags:**
- High waste (>12%)
- Low press utilization (<50%)
- No digital capability
- Single technology dependency
- Publishing-only focus

**Upside Indicators:**
- Packaging focus
- Digital press capability
- Web-to-print platform
- Long-term contracts
- Quality certifications
