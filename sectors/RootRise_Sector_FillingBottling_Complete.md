# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**$20-35B MENA beverage/filling market** with strong fundamentals in water, soft drinks, and growing functional beverage segments. Contract filling opportunity for SMEs without brand investment.

**SME Opportunities:**
- Contract/toll filling services
- Premium/artisanal water
- Functional beverages
- Private label for retailers
- Hospitality supply (Vision 2030)
- Regional/niche brands

**Success Factors:**
- Food safety certification (FSSC 22000)
- Line efficiency and uptime
- Quality consistency
- Cost management (packaging, utilities)
- Customer relationships

**Red Flags:**
- No GFSI certification
- Quality/recall issues
- Low OEE (<55%)
- Water source problems
- Single customer dependency

**Upside Indicators:**
- FSSC 22000 certified
- High OEE (>75%)
- Diversified customers
- Contract filling revenue
- Modern equipment
