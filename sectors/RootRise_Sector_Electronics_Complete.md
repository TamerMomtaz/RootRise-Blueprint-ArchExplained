# Document Summary

## Key Insights

**Market Characteristics:**
- $15-25B total MENA market
- High import competition (China)
- Infrastructure-driven demand
- Solar/renewable high growth
- Limited local R&D/design

**SME Opportunities:**
- Solar installation/systems
- Electrical contracting
- Cable distribution
- LED lighting
- Wiring devices

**Success Factors:**
- Quality/certification
- Cost competitiveness
- Technical capability
- Customer relationships
- Supply chain management

**Red Flags:**
- High defect rates (>3%)
- No certifications
- Single supplier dependency
- Old equipment
- No testing capability

**Upside Indicators:**
- Quality certifications
- Modern equipment
- Solar capability
- Project relationships
- Export capability
