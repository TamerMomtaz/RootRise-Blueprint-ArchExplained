# Summary

**$15-25B MENA market** with Saudi women's market transformation as major opportunity. Growing middle class and quality of life focus driving demand. Highly fragmented with consolidation opportunity.

**Success Factors:** Location, service quality, staff retention, digital presence, rebooking rate
**Red Flags:** High staff turnover, poor hygiene/compliance, no booking system, bad reviews
**Upside:** Premium positioning, membership model, multi-location, strong online presence
